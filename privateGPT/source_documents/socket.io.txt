Socket.IO

   Bidirectional and low-latency communication for every platform
   [https://socket.io/get-started/chat]Get
   started[https://socket.io/docs/v4/]Documentation

Performant

   In most cases, the connection will be established with WebSocket,
   providing a low-overhead communication channel between the server and
   the client.

Reliable

   Rest assured! In case the WebSocket connection is not possible, it will
   fall back to HTTP long-polling. And if the connection is lost, the
   client will automatically try to reconnect.

Scalable

   Scale to multiple servers and send events to all connected clients with
   ease.
