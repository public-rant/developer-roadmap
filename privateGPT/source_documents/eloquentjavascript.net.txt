Eloquent JavaScript

   3rd edition (2018)

   This is a book about JavaScript, programming, and the wonders of the
   digital. You can read it online here, or buy your own
   [https://nostarch.com/ejs3]paperback copy.

   Written by Marijn Haverbeke.

   Licensed under a
   [https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/3.0/]Creative Commons
   attribution-noncommercial license. All code in this book may also be
   considered licensed under an
   [https://eloquentjavascript.net/code/LICENSE]MIT license.

   Illustrations by various artists: Cover and chapter illustrations by
   [http://madalinatantareanu.com/]Madalina Tantareanu. Pixel art in
   Chapters 7 and 16 by Antonio Perdomo Pastor. Regular expression
   diagrams in Chapter 9 generated with
   [https://regexper.com/]regexper.com by Jeff Avallone. Village
   photograph in Chapter 11 by Fabrice Creuzot. Game concept for Chapter
   16 by [http://lessmilk.com/]Thomas Palef.

   The third edition was made possible by
   [https://eloquentjavascript.net/backers3.html]325 financial backers,
   most notably [https://nextjournal.com/]Nextjournal and
   [https://www.holbertonschool.com/]Holberton School . The second edition
   was supported by [https://eloquentjavascript.net/backers.html]454
   backers, with significant contributions from
   [https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/]Mozilla ,
   [https://www.hackreactor.com/]Hack Reactor , and
   [https://www.ghostery.com/]Ghostery .
